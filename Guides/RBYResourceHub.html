The RBY Resource Hub

Introduction

This is a conversion of my spreadsheet

A thing I noticed when entering RBY is that due to the community hopping, it tends to lose a lot of resources in the process. As a result, many players will often find themselves going to different websites here and there, tearing through old threads for stuff they're after. With this and a lot of other details in mind, I decided to take it upon myself to mine for resources across the communities known to have been used a lot.

There may be better ways to arrange this, but I'm currently going by website. This is due to some running their own formats, which can cause things to get a bit complicated when putting it all together. I have also ordered by date to gauge relevance to modern RBY. Use Ctrl+F for the formats you want or something. For easier navigation, I recommend using the Table of Contents to get to the list of site resources you're after, as they can be a bit long. If anyone has stuff to contribute, you can find me on RBY 2k20 or Smogon.

Tradebacks Tiering Survey Results

Introduction

RBY2k20 is not obligated to follow Smogon's naming conventions, but I had two different possible nomenclatures in mind for our tiering, so I thought it prudent to ask the community what they would prefer.

Results

Option Number of Responses
1U, 1U Banlist, 2U, 3U, etc. 10
Tier 1, Tier 1 Banlist, and abbreviated as T1, T2, T3, etc. 5
Other 3

There were 18 respondents to the survey. 10 respondents chose 1U, 1U Banlist, 2U, 3U, etc., which is 56% of all respondents. It is a clear majority, and so it is the nomenclature we will use for tiering on 2k20.
Here is a list of what people wrote when they selected Other:

1T, 1TBL, 2T, 3T, 4T (T is for "Tradeback")

Tradebacks OU, Tradebacks UU ,Tradebacks NU

1U, 2U, 3U (corresponding to smogon OU/UU/NU)
